Questions Answered

What does "compliance-ready" mean?

Everything we build has audit trails, role-based access, and data residency controls from day one. This isn't retrofitted — it's designed in from the ground up. Every document processed, every query made, every response generated is logged immutably for regulatory inspection.

Who is this for?

Mid-sized regulated firms in India and Europe — CA firms, NBFCs, asset managers, boutique law firms. These firms are too large for generic tools and too small to build custom AI solutions. They're exactly the segment that gets ignored by Big Tech but has real budget for compliance solutions.

Is my data safe?

Your data never leaves your infrastructure. Deploy on-premise or in your VPC. We offer AWS eu-central-1 (Frankfurt) for EU clients and AWS ap-south-1 (Mumbai) for India. We never train on your data — it's a contractual commitment, not just a promise.

How much does it cost?

Pricing starts at ₹8,000/month for Compliance Intelligence in India (Keystone/Nexus), and €800/month for EU markets. Atlas is priced per-engagement (₹30,000-50,000). Custom enterprise plans available for on-premise deployments. Design partner program is free for qualified firms.

Can I try before I buy?

Yes — we offer 60-day design partner programs for qualified firms. No cost, honest feedback in exchange for product shaping. It's how we build products that actually solve real problems. We learn from your use cases, you get early access to tools that fit your workflow.

What about EU AI Act?

We monitor every regulatory update. Our products are designed to meet EU AI Act requirements for high-risk AI systems (financial services, legal services). When the full enforcement begins in August 2026, we'll be ready. This is a key differentiator — most AI vendors are scrambling to catch up.
